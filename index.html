What is Loeys-Dietz syndrome?

Loeys-Dietz syndrome (LDS) is a rare genetic condition that affects connective tissue in the body. It can impact the heart, blood vessels, bones, joints, skin, and more.

Brief Facts

  • Caused by changes in genes related to the TGF-beta pathway
  • Often affects the aorta and arteries
  • May cause scoliosis, joint flexibility, and easy bruising
  • Diagnosed through genetic testing and imaging
